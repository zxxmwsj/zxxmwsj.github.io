Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how xcwyb.com (hereinafter referred to as “this Website”) handles your information when you visit xcwyb.com or read investment research. This Website adheres to the principles of necessity, transparency, and data minimization: information is processed only when necessary to provide website functionality, maintain security, understand content usage, or fulfill legal obligations.

Information We May Collect

Information You Provide

When you contact the author via email or engage in other interactions, you may provide your name or nickname, email address, personal website, message content, or attachments. Please do not submit identity documents, home addresses, passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal information in public comments.

Access and Device Information

To process web requests, this Website’s hosting service provider and content delivery network (CDN) may automatically receive information such as your IP address, access time, requested pages, referring pages, browser type, operating system, language settings, and error logs. This information may be used for service delivery, troubleshooting, performance improvement, and security purposes.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

This Website, its themes, comment systems, analytics service providers, or advertising service providers may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to remember settings, support features, measure usage, or serve advertisements. You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings, though doing so may cause certain features to malfunction.

How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve this Website and its features;

  • Publish and manage comments and respond to inquiries;

  • Detect abnormal traffic, reduce spam, and protect this Website and its users;

  • Understand traffic patterns, content usage, and website performance;

  • Comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or lawful requests from relevant authorities.

This Website does not operate for the purpose of selling personal information.

Third-Party Services

Depending on the actual configuration of this Website, the following types of third-party services may be used:

  • Analytics Services: Such as Ahrefs Analytics, Google Analytics, or similar tools, used to understand traffic, referral sources, performance, and usage trends. The specific services actually loaded on this website determine which data is processed;

Third-party services have their own privacy policies and data control measures. This website has no control over how these providers independently process information. Please review their policies before using the relevant features. If a service is not actually enabled, the content in this section does not apply to this website’s actual data collection practices.

Sharing and Disclosure

This website may provide limited information to necessary providers of hosting, infrastructure, analytics, advertising, or commenting services, or disclose information as required by law, in order to:

  1. Provide hosting, transmission, commenting, analytics, security, or advertising functions;

  2. Investigate spam, fraud, attacks, infringement, or other forms of abuse;

  3. Comply with legal requirements, legal proceedings, or lawful government requests;

  4. Support the transfer, merger, or reorganization of this website’s assets, where permitted by law.

Content you post in public comments is not considered private information by this website. Please ensure you consent to other visitors viewing this content before submitting it.

Retention and Security

Information may be retained for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, after which it will be deleted, anonymized, or no longer used. Retention periods for comments, backups, server logs, and third-party services may vary. This website employs reasonable measures for access control, configuration management, and transmission security; however, absolute security cannot be guaranteed for any internet transmission or cloud service.
