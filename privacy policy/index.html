privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how xcwyb (referred to as “the Site”) may handle information when you visit xcwyb.com, read investment research and market commentary, use comments, or contact the author. The Site follows the principles of necessity, transparency, and data minimization: information is processed only as needed to provide site functionality, maintain security, understand content usage, or comply with legal obligations.

Information we may collect

Information you provide

When you post a comment, contact the author by email, or participate in another interaction, you may provide a name or nickname, email address, personal website, message content, or attachments. Please do not submit identity documents, home addresses, passwords, payment details, or other sensitive personal information in public comments.

Visit and device information

To deliver web requests, the Site’s hosting provider and CDN may automatically receive information such as your IP address, visit time, requested page, referring page, browser type, operating system, language settings, and error logs. This information may be used for delivery, troubleshooting, performance improvement, and security.

Cookies and similar technologies

The Site, its theme, comment systems, analytics providers, or advertising providers may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to remember settings, support functionality, measure usage, or deliver advertisements. You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings, although some features may not work properly as a result.

How we use information

We may use information to:

  • provide, maintain, and improve the Site and its features;
  • publish and manage comments and respond to inquiries;
  • detect abnormal traffic, reduce spam, and protect the Site and its users;
  • understand traffic patterns, content usage, and site performance;
  • comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or lawful requests from authorities.

The Site does not operate as a business that sells personal information, and ordinary reading does not require an account.

Third-party services

Depending on the Site’s actual configuration, it may use the following types of third-party services:

  • Analytics services: such as Ahrefs Analytics, Google Analytics, Baidu Analytics, Cloudflare Web Analytics, or similar tools to understand traffic, referrals, performance, and usage trends. The services actually loaded by the Site determine what data is processed;
  • Advertising services: the Site may use Google AdSense or similar advertising tools. Providers may use cookies or similar technologies to measure advertising performance or display advertisements based on visit, device, and page information;
  • Comment services: if Waline, Twikoo, Giscus, Valine, or another comment system is enabled, the comment content and submitted nickname, email address, or website may be processed by that provider;
  • Content and infrastructure services: fonts, images, code hosting, CDNs, social sharing, or embedded content may be provided by third parties that receive request, IP address, or device information.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies and data controls. The Site does not control how those providers independently process information. Please review their policies before using a related feature. If a service is not actually enabled, this section does not expand the Site’s actual collection practices.

Sharing and disclosure

The Site may provide limited information to necessary hosting, infrastructure, analytics, advertising, or comment providers, or disclose information as required by law, in order to:

  1. provide hosting, transmission, comments, analytics, security, or advertising features;
  2. investigate spam, fraud, attacks, infringement, or other misuse;
  3. comply with legal requirements, legal process, or lawful government requests;
  4. support a transfer, merger, or restructuring of the Site’s assets where legally permitted.

Content that you publish in a public comment is not treated as private information by the Site. Please make sure you are comfortable with other visitors seeing it before submitting.

Retention and security

Information may be retained for as long as necessary for the purposes described above, then deleted, anonymized, or no longer used. Retention periods may differ for comments, backups, server logs, and third-party services. The Site uses reasonable access controls, configuration management, and transmission security measures, but no internet transmission or cloud service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices and rights

You may:

  • manage or delete cookies through your browser settings;
  • request correction or deletion of a comment or contact information you submitted;
  • ask whether the Site processes information related to you and request an explanation of that processing;
  • decline non-essential analytics or personalized advertising through browser, advertising-service, or third-party opt-out controls.

To make a request, contact contact@xcwyb.com and describe what you need, together with reasonable information to help us verify the request. We may need to verify your identity to protect the Site and other people’s privacy. Information that must be retained by law may be excluded.

Children

The Site is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. Minors should use the Site with a parent or guardian’s guidance. Please do not submit a child’s personal information in comments or messages. If a parent or guardian believes that such information has been submitted, please contact us so it can be reviewed.

International access

The Site may use hosting, CDN, analytics, advertising, and comment services located in different countries or regions. Visiting the Site or using these features may transfer information outside your location, where it will be handled under the relevant provider’s policy.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Policy may be updated as the Site, its third-party services, or applicable requirements change. Material changes will be described on this page, and the revised Policy will take effect when published. Please review this page periodically.

Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or data concerns, email contact@xcwyb.com.

Before publishing, confirm that this email address is controlled by the site owner. Update the third-party services section to match the analytics, advertising, comment, CDN, and hosting services actually enabled on the Site.
